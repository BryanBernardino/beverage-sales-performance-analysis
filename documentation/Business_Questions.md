# Business Questions

## Project Goal

The objective of this dashboard is to transform raw beverage sales data into meaningful business insights that support decision-making.

---

## Questions Answered

### Sales Performance

- What is the company's total revenue?
- How many units have been sold?
- How many customer orders have been completed?

### Product Performance

- Which beverage category generates the highest revenue?
- Which products are the top-selling items?
- Which products contribute the least revenue?

### Trend Analysis

- How has revenue changed from 2021 to 2023?
- Is the business experiencing consistent growth?

### Regional Performance

- Which regions contribute the highest revenue?
- Which regions have opportunities for improvement?

### Business Decisions

- Which products should receive additional marketing investment?
- Which product categories should maintain higher inventory levels?
