# Business Insights

## Overall Sales Performance

The dashboard reports a total revenue of approximately **1.18 Billion**, generated from more than **208 Million** units sold across nearly **3 Million** customer orders.

---

## Product Performance

Alcoholic beverages generated the highest revenue among all product categories, making them the primary revenue driver.

---

## Sales Trend

Sales increased steadily from **2021** to **2023**, indicating consistent business growth over the observed period.

---

## Regional Analysis

Revenue is distributed across multiple regions, with several regions contributing significantly more than others. This information can be used to prioritize marketing and inventory allocation.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Recommendation 1

Increase inventory levels for high-performing beverage categories to prevent stock shortages.

## Recommendation 2

Analyze lower-performing products to determine whether pricing, promotions, or product positioning should be adjusted.

## Recommendation 3

Focus marketing campaigns on regions with strong sales performance while developing strategies to improve lower-performing regions.

## Recommendation 4

Continue monitoring yearly sales trends to support future forecasting and business planning.
